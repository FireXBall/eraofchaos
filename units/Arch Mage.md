## General information
 **Description:** Mage is a title only given to the most powerful spellcasters. Their presence in battle gives their side great magical advantage.

 **Class:** [Caster](/units/Unit Class Caster)

 **Class Description:** With their mastery of the secrets of magic, Caster units have higher magic resistance.

 **Faction:** [Tower](/units/Fraction Tower)

 **Race:** Human

 **Members:** [x4](/units/Unit Member x4)

 **Rank:** [SR](/units/Unit Rank SR)

 **Short description:** Magic unit with soaking shield

 **Position :** Crowd control your enemies' back row troops, enhance your heroes' spells, and reduce opponent heroes' MP restore.

 **Recommend:** Deals extra damage to any slowed enemies.
